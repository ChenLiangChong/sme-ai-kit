# PMM Gate 7: Launch Execution

Tier-based launch planning, launch day checklist, stakeholder coordination, and post-launch monitoring.

---

## Launch Tiers

| Tier | Scope | Prep Time | Budget | Team |
|------|-------|-----------|--------|------|
| 1 | New product, major feature | 6-8 weeks | $50-100k | Full cross-functional |
| 2 | Significant feature, integration | 3-4 weeks | $10-25k | Marketing + Product |
| 3 | Small improvement | 1 week | <$5k | Marketing only |

---

## Tier 1 Launch Workflow

Execute major product launch:

1. Kickoff meeting with Product, Marketing, Sales, CS
2. Define goals (pipeline $, MQLs, press coverage, signups)
3. Develop positioning and messaging (Gates 2-3)
4. Create sales enablement (deck, demo, battlecard)
5. Build campaign assets (landing page, emails, ads)
6. Train sales and CS teams
7. Execute launch day (press, email, ads, outbound)
8. Monitor and optimize for 30 days
9. **Validation:** Pipeline on track to goal by week 2

### Week-by-Week Timeline (Tier 1)

| Week | Focus | Key Deliverables |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| T-8 | Kickoff | Goals, team, timeline confirmed |
| T-7 | Research | Competitive update, persona validation |

| T-0 | Launch Day | Execute launch playbook |

## Launch Day Checklist

### Pre-Launch (Morning)

- [ ] Final product smoke test passed
- [ ] All URLs and links verified
- [ ] Support team briefed and ready
- [ ] War Room / communication channel active
- [ ] Rollback plan confirmed
- [ ] Go/No-Go decision made

### Launch Execution

- [ ] Press release distributed
- [ ] Email announcement sent
- [ ] Social media posts live (all platforms)
- [ ] Paid ads activated at full budget
- [ ] In-app notification/banner active
- [ ] Sales outbound blitz launched
- [ ] Product Hunt / community posts (if applicable)
- [ ] Partner notifications sent

### Post-Launch (Same Day)

- [ ] Metrics monitored every 2 hours
- [ ] All social comments responded to
- [ ] Bug reports triaged
- [ ] End-of-day report sent to stakeholders

### Go/No-Go Decision Framework (Pre-Launch)

| Signal | GO | NO-GO |
|--------|----|-------|
| Product stability | < 1% error rate | > 5% error rate |
| Core feature tested | All critical paths pass | Any critical path fails |
| Sales readiness | Team trained + materials ready | Not briefed |
| Support readiness | FAQ + playbook ready | No preparation |
| Legal/Compliance | All cleared | Any outstanding |
| Marketing assets | Core assets complete | Missing critical assets |

### Rollback Criteria (Post-Launch)

- Critical bug affecting > 10% of users
- Security vulnerability discovered
- Legal/compliance issue identified
- Infrastructure cannot handle load
- Core value proposition not functioning as promised
- Severe negative press/social backlash

## Post-Launch Retrospective

### Schedule

| Retrospective | When | Duration | Focus |
|--------------|------|----------|-------|
| Hot debrief | Day 1 | 30 min | Immediate fixes needed |
| Week 1 retro | Day 7 | 60 min | Tactical adjustments |
| 30-day retro | Day 30 | 90 min | Strategy assessment |
| 90-day retro | Day 90 | 90 min | Full impact evaluation |

### Retro Template

1. **Results vs. Goals**: How did we do against targets?
2. **What worked**: What should we repeat?
3. **What didn't work**: What should we change?
4. **Surprises**: What did we not expect?
5. **Action items**: What do we do next? (owner + deadline)

## SaaS Launch Strategy Supplement

Core philosophy: A great launch is a campaign, not an announcement.

### Task-Specific Questions

1. What are you launching? (New product, major feature, minor update)
2. What's your current audience size and engagement?
3. What owned channels do you have? (Email list size, blog traffic, community)
4. What's your timeline for launch?
5. Have you launched before? What worked/didn't work?
6. Are you considering Product Hunt? What's your preparation status?

### Proactive Triggers

1. **Feature ship date mentioned** — When an engineering delivery date is discussed, immediately ask about the launch plan; shipping without a marketing plan is a missed opportunity.
2. **Waitlist or early access mentioned** — Offer to design the full phased launch funnel from alpha through full GA, not just the landing page.
3. **Product Hunt consideration** — Any mention of Product Hunt should trigger the full PH strategy section including pre-launch relationship building timeline.
4. **Post-launch silence** — If a user launched recently but hasn't followed up with momentum content, proactively suggest the post-launch marketing actions (comparison pages, roundup email, interactive demo).
5. **Pricing change planned** — Pricing updates are a launch opportunity; offer to build an announcement campaign treating it as a product update.

### Output Artifacts

| Artifact | Format | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Launch Plan | Markdown doc | Phase-by-phase plan with owners, dates, channels, and success metrics |
| ORB Channel Map | Table | Owned/Rented/Borrowed channel strategy with tactics per channel |
| Launch Day Checklist | Checklist | Complete day-of execution checklist with time-boxed actions |
| Product Hunt Brief | Markdown doc | Listing copy, asset specs, pre-launch timeline, engagement playbook |
| Post-Launch Momentum Plan | Bulleted list | 30-day post-launch actions to sustain and compound the launch |

## 台灣中小企業草根上市策略

> 以下策略適用於預算有限的台灣中小企業，不需要大型行銷團隊或高額預算。

### 低成本驗證路線

| 階段 | 做什麼 | 預算 |
|------|--------|------|
| 1. 社群測水溫 | FB 相關社團發問/投票，測試需求 | 免費 |
| 2. LINE 早鳥 | 用 LINE OA 推播忠實客戶，開放早鳥預購 | 免費（既有好友） |
| 3. 微網紅試用 | 寄送 3-5 位微網紅公關品，換取開箱內容 | NT$5,000-15,000（產品成本） |
| 4. 正式上架 | 蝦皮/自有通路上架，搭配 Meta 廣告投放 | NT$10,000-30,000/月 |

### 群眾募資（適用於實體新產品）

台灣群募平台（嘖嘖 zeczec、挖貝 WaBay）適合：
- 3C、設計小物、創新家電、環保產品
- 同時達成市場驗證 + 首波資金 + 公關曝光
- 注意：需要 30-60 天的預熱期和高品質的專案頁面

### 台灣上市 vs 歐美上市的差異

| 面向 | 歐美 SaaS 上市 | 台灣中小企業上市 |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| 核心平台 | Product Hunt, Hacker News | FB 社團、LINE OA、蝦皮 |
| 預算 | $50,000-100,000 | NT$10,000-50,000 |
| 破冰方式 | 冷信+PR+付費廣告 | 口碑+KOL試用+社群預熱 |
| 成功指標 | MQL, pipeline $ | 首月銷量、好友增長、評價數 |
